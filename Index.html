What is S.E.A

S.E.A is an international school for children from KG through K twelve (AS and A-Level). S.E.A provides standard education to every child. With the help of hundreds of professional teachers, we are one of the top schools in Asia.

S.E.A was found by Mr and Mrs. Ei Sulai Aung in 1994. The school gained the title of "best school of the year" in its first 2 years.

S.E.A focus on making career paths for every child, teaching how to deal with problems in real life, and being ready to face the world and beyond.
